The following policy shall be included in the Rules of Order adopted by the Core Working Group. 

1. The Core Working Group shall not approve any Technical Specification or Report without the consent of the Responsible Working Group Chair, or any chair of a working group that originated the paper if the Responsible Working Group Chair is not present.
2. The Core Working Group shall not approve or consider approval of any Technical Specification or Report unless at least one Chair of a Working Group which originated the paper is present.
